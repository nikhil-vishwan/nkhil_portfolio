Help & OHAT Guide
OHAT Scoring Reference
0 = Healthy 1 = Monitor 2 = Urgent
👄 Lips: 0–Pink moist · 1–Dry chapped · 2–Swelling ulcers
👅 Tongue: 0–Pink clean · 1–Patchy coating · 2–Red sore thrush
🦷 Gums: 0–Pink firm · 1–Red puffy · 2–Abscess bleeding
💧 Saliva: 0–Moist watery · 1–Dry sticky · 2–None present
🦷 Teeth: 0–No decay · 1–1-3 decay · 2–4+ / broken
🦷 Dentures: 0–Clean fit labelled · 1–Loose worn · 2–Broken missing
🪥 Hygiene: 0–Clean · 1–1-2 areas · 2–Heavy plaque
😣 Pain: 0–None · 1–Behavioral signs · 2–Physical signs
Total Score Actions
✅ Score 0–3: Healthy
Maintain standard daily oral care routine.
⚠️ Score 4–8: Monitor
Increase brushing to 3× daily. Re-check in 72 hours.
🚨 Score 9+: RED FLAG
Notify Lead Nurse immediately for dental referral.
